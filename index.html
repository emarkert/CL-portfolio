Hi, I'm Evan.

I'm a learning UI/UX Designer.

Please pardon my dust -- This portfolio is most certainly under construction while I learn some of the finer aspects of front-end web development.

I am very passionate about UI and UX Design to bring accessible and enjoyable experiences to end-users and working in a collaborative and enthusiastic team to accomplish these goals. I'm currently working with Code Louisville in Louisville, KY to develop and sharpen these skills while supplementing my education from all the various sources on the web.
